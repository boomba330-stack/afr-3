Vinyl sleeves serve as protective covers that shield the records from dust, scratches, and damage while also providing a canvas for artwork and design that enhances the listening experience.

They help in identifying the album or soundtrack, often featuring the title, artist, and unique visual themes that reflect the music inside.

Sleeves also make storing and organizing records easier, ensuring that each vinyl stays secure and well-preserved over time.

In collections, they add an aesthetic and collectible value, turning the vinyl into both a functional music format and a piece of visual art.
